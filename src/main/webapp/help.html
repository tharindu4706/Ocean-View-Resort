How to Use the System

1. Login

Enter your username and password on the login page

Default credentials: admin / admin123

2. Add New Guest

Click on "Guests" in the menu

Fill in guest details: name, address, contact, email

Click "Add Guest" button

3. Create Reservation

Click on "Reservations" in the menu

Select guest and room

Choose check-in and check-out dates

Click "Create Reservation"

4. View Room Availability

Click on "Rooms" to see all rooms

Green = Available, Red = Occupied

5. Calculate Bill

Go to reservation details

Click "View Bill"

System automatically calculates total cost

6. Logout

Click "Logout" button in the header
